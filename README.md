## U.S. Export Destinations

I modified the Census trade data code to examine the top 10 destinations for U.S. exports in 2015 and 2025. The graph shows that Canada and Mexico remained the two largest destinations for U.S. exports in both years. A notable change is that Mexico became much closer to Canada by 2025, suggesting that North American trade became even more central to U.S. export patterns.

China remained the third-largest destination, but it was far below Canada and Mexico in both years. The 2025 list also includes Switzerland and Taiwan, while Hong Kong and Belgium were in the 2015 top 10 but not in the 2025 top 10.
